We help technical founders become commercial leaders.

UPNY Ventures works alongside PhD researchers, scientists, engineers, and technical founders from day one — building the commercial proof points, customer traction, and go-to-market infrastructure that transform university-developed technologies into venture-scale companies.

The Go-To-Market Program

Phase 1
GTM Strategy Course
2 Weeks · Free to Public
Open to All Founders
Every engagement starts with strategy. This free 2-week course is open to any technical founder — PhD researchers, scientists, engineers, and deep-tech innovators — and builds the GTM foundation: identifying your ideal customer profile, mapping priority verticals, sharpening your value proposition, and building the execution workflows you'll need.

Advancement to Phase 2 is selective. Founders are evaluated on completion and quality. Not everyone advances. That's by design.
Phase 2
100-Day Challenge
GTM Commercialization Engine
Top Performing Founders
The 100-Day Challenge turns strategy into a company. Weekly execution sprints — virtual and in-person — drive founders through expanding customer discovery, building outreach at scale, and testing their value proposition against real buyers.

The goal by the end of the 100 days: early validation outcomes — a letter of intent, a signed pilot, a paying early adopter. Proof that the market is responding. Founders are scored throughout.
Phase 3
Pre-Seed Investment
$50–100K
Top Performers
The top performers from Phases 1 and 2 qualify for a first pre-seed check. Selection is based on scores and demonstrated commercial traction — not pitch quality or founder pedigree.

This is the first institutional capital — deployed to move from validated market interest to early customers and revenue.
Phase 4
Pre-Seed Follow-on
$100K
Scaling Companies
Companies that deploy Phase 3 capital effectively and continue to demonstrate traction qualify for a follow-on pre-seed investment.

This is the scaling check — capital to build on early wins, grow the customer base, and build the proof points that make a Seed round possible.

How We Evaluate

01
Team
Grit is criterion one. We're looking to validate companies with early-customer traction and then scale them.
02
Problem
Large, real, and durable. We back solutions to problems that don't go away — the kind that, if solved, fundamentally change the economics or capabilities of an industry.
03
Technology
Incubated or accelerated, and defensible. Genuine technical differentiation that creates lasting competitive advantage.
04
Early Signal
A customer conversation. A pilot interest. A signed LOI. We invest before revenue, but not before evidence. Some indication that the market is listening.
05
Stage
Pre-seed. Post-lab and incubation. We back NY founders who are ready to take their product to early customers and grow from there.
