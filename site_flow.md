## Section 3 — GTM Program

**Purpose:** Prove the model. Show founders and investors the program is real, structured, and selective.

**Content:**

[LABEL] GTM Program

From incubated to Seed-ready.

---

Phase 1 — GTM Strategy Course · 2 weeks · Free to public

Open to all founders. Builds the GTM foundation: ideal customer profile, priority verticals, value proposition, and the workflows to execute. Participation in Phase 2 is selective — evaluated on completion and quality. Not everyone advances.

---

Phase 2 — 100-Day Challenge · GTM Commercialization Engine

100 days of structured execution. Weekly sprints drive founders through customer discovery, outreach at scale, and value proposition testing against real buyers. Goal: early validation outcomes — a letter of intent, a signed pilot, a paying early adopter. Scored throughout.

---

Phase 3 — Pre-Seed Investment · $25–50K

Top performers from Phases 1 and 2 qualify for a first pre-seed check. Selection based on scores and demonstrated commercial traction. Capital to move from validated interest to early customers and revenue.

---

Phase 4 — Pre-Seed Follow-on Investment · $100K

Companies that deploy Phase 3 capital effectively and continue to demonstrate traction qualify for a follow-on investment. The scaling check — builds on early wins toward a fundable Seed round.

---

How We Evaluate Companies

01 — Team
Grit is criterion one. We can teach go-to-market. We cannot teach resilience. We back founders who keep going when the science doesn't cooperate and the market doesn't respond on schedule.

02 — Problem
Large, real, and durable. Solutions to problems that don't go away — the kind that, if solved, fundamentally change an industry.

03 — Technology
University-incubated and defensible. Genuine technical differentiation that takes years to replicate.

04 — Early Signal
A customer conversation. A pilot interest. A signed LOI. We invest before revenue, but not before evidence.

05 — Stage
Pre-seed. Post-incubation. Before Seed. This is exactly where we invest — and where almost no one else in Upstate New York is focused.

[LABEL] Invest

$5M Fund I

UPNY is raising its first fund to back 20 deep-tech companies at pre-seed across Upstate New York. New York State has invested heavily in the research infrastructure that produces these founders. The pipeline is real. The missing piece has always been what comes after the incubator — the first institutional capital, paired with the commercial support to get to Seed. Fund I is that piece.

---

01 — Angel
Individual investors seeking early-stage access to incubated and accelerated deep-tech startups. We target investments of $10K–$50K alongside experienced operators and advisors who contribute mentorship, guidance, and strategic support within portfolio companies. Our goal is to strengthen the entrepreneurial ecosystem through active participation and founder development.

02 — Institutional
Family offices and institutional LPs seeking exposure to a structured pre-seed investment vehicle backed by operational support and commercialization strategy. Our approach is to establish validation through a growing network of angels, founders, and statewide partnerships that create the foundation for long-term institutional participation and scalable fund growth.

03 — Partnerships
New York State programs and economic development organizations focused on deep-tech commercialization, regional growth, and translating publicly funded research into market-ready companies. We seek aligned partnerships that support entrepreneurship, accelerate innovation, and strengthen the long-term startup infrastructure across Upstate New York.
