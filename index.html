Mission

Every year in New York State, founders leave university labs, incubators & accelerators with solutions to complex problems. The technology will work but there is a gap between proof of concept and the revenue needed for proof of marketability. The feedback is "Not VC ready. No customers." That is a fair verdict.
UPNY Ventures is purpose-built to help these founders fill that gap.

We work alongside New York State deep-tech founders to establish early-customer traction and market validation that lead to additional investment rounds and sustainable growth.

Angel
Individual investors seeking early-stage access to incubated and accelerated deep-tech startups. We target investments of $10K–$50K alongside experienced operators and advisors who contribute mentorship, guidance, and strategic support within portfolio companies. Our goal is to strengthen the entrepreneurial ecosystem through active participation and founder development.
Institutional
Family offices and institutional LPs seeking exposure to a structured pre-seed investment vehicle backed by operational support and commercialization strategy. Our approach is to establish validation through a growing network of angels, founders, and statewide partnerships that create the foundation for long-term institutional participation and scalable fund growth.
Partnerships
New York State programs and economic development organizations focused on deep-tech commercialization, regional growth, and translating publicly funded research into market-ready companies. We're actively looking to foster aligned partnerships that support entrepreneurship, accelerate innovation, and strengthen the long-term startup infrastructure across Upstate.
