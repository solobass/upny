# UPNY Ventures — Context for Claude

## What UPNY Is
Pre-seed venture fund based in Albany, NY. Backs university-incubated deep-tech founders in New York State with two things: **pre-seed capital** ($50–100K, with $100K follow-on) and **hands-on GTM support** via a structured 4-phase commercialization program.

Founding Partner: **Allan Von Schenkel** — five-time founder, 20 years scaling software companies.

Website: upny.vc | Email: info@upny.vc

## LinkedIn Post Strategy

### Three Psychographic Audiences

**1. Investors (LPs)**
- Motivation: Early access, asymmetric upside, part of something before it's obvious
- Fear: Missing the window, no deal flow visibility
- Angle: NYS deep tech is underinvested, the window is now, UPNY is structured and raising Fund I

**2. Founders**
- Motivation: Getting to market, proving the work matters, finding someone who gets deep-tech timelines
- Fear: Hearing "not VC ready" again, not knowing how to sell
- Angle: We come in before the customers and build toward them alongside you

**3. Partners / Ecosystem**
- Motivation: Strengthening the region, collaboration, not duplicating effort
- Key orgs: Launch NY, CEG, Upstate Capital, upstate venture community, universities (SUNY, Cornell, RPI, UAlbany, Clarkson)
- Angle: UPNY as connective tissue in the Upstate ecosystem — amplifying, not competing
