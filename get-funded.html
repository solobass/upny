UPNY Ventures backs Deep-Tech NYS founders at the earliest stage — before customers, before revenue — with Pre-Seed Investment and a structured GTM Program designed to get them there.

GTM Program

Phase 1
Strategy
6 Week Bootcamp
100+ Startups
Every engagement starts with structure. Founders work through a focused GTM planning process — identifying their ideal customer profile, mapping priority verticals, sharpening their value proposition, and building the workflows they'll use to execute. The goal at the end of Phase 1: a clear, actionable plan and the commitment to work it.

Participation is selective. Founders are scored on completion and quality. Not everyone advances.
Phase 2
Activate
12 Week Commercialization Engine
Top 25+ Startups
The Commercialization Engine puts the plan into action. Weekly execution sprints — virtual and in-person — drive founders through expanding customer discovery, building outreach at scale, and testing their value proposition against real buyers.

The goal by the end of Phase 2: early validation outcomes — a letter of intent, a signed pilot, a paying early adopter. Proof that the market is responding. Founders are scored throughout.
Phase 3
Pre-Seed Investment
Top Scoring Startups
The top performers from Phase 1 and Phase 2 qualify for pre-seed funding consideration. Selection is based on scores and demonstrated traction.

This is the bridge — from incubated startup to funded, growth-stage company, with the customers, pilots, and proof points to raise a Seed round.

How We Evaluate

01
Team
Grit is criterion one. Deep-tech commercialization is a long, non-linear road. We back founders who keep going when the science doesn't cooperate and the market doesn't respond on schedule. We can teach go-to-market. We cannot teach resilience.
02
Problem
Large, real, and durable. We back solutions to problems that don't go away — the kind that, if solved, fundamentally change the economics or capabilities of an industry.
03
Technology
Incubated or accelerated, and defensible. Genuine technical differentiation that creates lasting competitive advantage.
04
Early Signal
A customer conversation. A pilot interest. A signed LOI. We invest before revenue, but not before evidence. Some indication that the market is listening.
05
Stage
Pre-seed. Post-lab and incubation. We invest in disruptive products not services — and in NY founders.
