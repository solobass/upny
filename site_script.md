## [SECTION 4 — GTM PROGRAM]
*The differentiator. 3 phases + funding criteria.*

```
LABEL: GTM Program

HEADLINE:
From incubated to investable.
In four phases.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

PHASE 1 — GTM Strategy Course     [ 2 weeks · Free to public ]

Open to all founders. Builds the GTM foundation: target customers, priority
verticals, value proposition, and the execution workflows needed to go to
market. Advancement to Phase 2 is selective — founders are evaluated on
completion and quality. Not everyone advances.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

PHASE 2 — 100-Day Challenge       [ GTM Commercialization Engine ]

100 days of structured execution. Weekly sprints drive founders through
customer discovery at scale, pipeline building, and value proposition testing
against real buyers. The goal: early validation outcomes. LOIs. Signed pilots.
Proof that someone will pay. Founders are scored throughout.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

PHASE 3 — Pre-Seed Investment     [ $25–50K ]

The top performers qualify for a first pre-seed check. Selection is based on
scores and demonstrated commercial traction. Capital to move from validated
market interest to early customers and revenue.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

PHASE 4 — Pre-Seed Follow-on      [ $100K ]

Companies that deploy Phase 3 capital effectively and demonstrate continued
traction qualify for a $100K follow-on. The scaling check — builds toward
a fundable Seed round.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

FUNDING CRITERIA

01  Team            We back founders with the grit to keep going when
                    the science doesn't cooperate and the market doesn't
                    respond. Grit is criterion one.

02  Problem         Is the problem large, real, and durable? We want
                    to back solutions to problems that don't go away.

03  Technology      University-incubated. Defensible. Not a feature —
                    a foundation.

04  Early Signal    A customer conversation. A pilot interest. A signed
                    LOI. Some evidence that the market is responding.

05  Stage           Pre-seed. Post-incubation. Before Series A.
                    This is exactly where we invest.
```

## NARRATIVE THREAD (how the page reads as a story)

1. **Landing** — establishes identity, stops the scroll
2. **Mission** — opens with the problem (pitch room, "not VC ready"), positions UPNY as the answer
3. **What We Do** — sharpens the positioning: capital + GTM, the bottleneck is GTM
4. **GTM Program** — proves the claim with a structured, credible program + funding criteria
5. **Media** — builds ongoing credibility, keeps the site alive
6. **Invest** — makes the LP ask after the thesis has landed
7. **About** — the human behind it; closes the loop on the founding moment
8. **Contact** — clean split CTA: founders and investors get separate paths

**Primary reader (investor) arc:**
*Problem they recognize → differentiated solution → credible program → LP structure → credible founder → clear ask*
