The Portfolio Path

Year 1
Activate
Customer Discovery & Early Validation
Founders complete the GTM Program — refining their ICP, building outreach infrastructure, and running their first real customer conversations. The goal: early signal. A letter of intent, a pilot interest, a paying early adopter. Proof that the market is listening.
Year 2
Convert
Pilots, Contracts & Early Revenue
Customer conversations become paying relationships. Signed pilots, contracted early adopters, and the first revenue milestones — the proof points needed to open the next conversation with seed-stage investors.
Year 3
Scale
$1M+ ARR Target
With a repeatable sales motion and validated value proposition, portfolio companies push toward their first significant revenue milestone — the foundation for a Series A narrative.
Year 4
Raise or Exit
Series A or Acquisition
Companies with revenue, retention, and a clear growth trajectory are positioned to raise a Series A or enter acquisition conversations. UPNY actively supports downstream fundraising introductions and M&A readiness.
Year 5
Return
LP Distributions
Liquidity events — acquisitions, secondary transactions, and downstream rounds — generate returns to LP investors. The fund is designed to produce meaningful early distributions through the portfolio's most successful outcomes.

Who Invests With Us

01
Angel Investors
Individual investors seeking early-stage access to incubated and accelerated deep-tech startups. New York State programs and economic development organizations focused on deep-tech commercialization, regional growth, and translating publicly funded research into market-ready companies. UPNY is purpose-built to align with — and amplify — existing NYS investment in technology and entrepreneurship.
